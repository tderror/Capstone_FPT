BUOC 1 — AST Chunking & Risk Detection

Phan tich cu phap ma nguon Solidity, trich xuat cau truc va nhan dien cac ham co rui ro.

ast_parser.py tree-sitter-solidity regex fallback

Thu tu uu tien phan tich:

  • tree-sitter-solidity (chinh): Nhanh, chinh xac, khong can bien dich. Su dung WASM parser de phan tich cu phap AST.
  • Regex (du phong): Dung brace counting (ton trong strings/comments) de trich xuat ham co ban.

Thong tin trich xuat:

  • Contracts: Ten, loai (contract/interface/library/abstract), ke thua, pham vi dong
  • Functions: Ten, visibility (public/external/internal/private), mutability, modifiers, parameters
  • State Variables: Ten, kieu du lieu, visibility, co phai mapping/array
  • Modifiers: nonReentrant, onlyOwner, whenNotPaused...

Risk Indicators (phat hien rui ro):

  • has_external_call: Phat hien .call{value:}, .delegatecall, .send(), .transfer()
  • has_state_change: Cap nhat mapping, ghi vao storage
  • potential_reentrancy: Goi ben ngoai + thay doi trang thai (khong co mutex)
  • no_reentrancy_guard: Khong co modifier nonReentrant tren ham co external call
  • uses_tx_origin: Su dung tx.origin (SWC-115)
  • has_delegatecall: Su dung delegatecall (rui ro cao)

Output — ASTResult:

// Ket qua phan tich AST { "solidity_version": "^0.4.24", "contracts": [Contract{name, functions[], state_variables[], modifiers[]}], "total_lines": 120, "parse_method": "tree-sitter" | "regex" } // Function chunk (cho RAG search): { "name": "withdraw", "contract": "Bank", "risk_indicators": ["external call", "state update", "no guard"], "has_external_call": true, "has_state_change": true, "priority": 3 // so risk indicators, de sap xep }

BUOC 2 + 3 — Slither & RAG Search (SONG SONG)

Hai buoc logic doc lap, chay dong thoi bang asyncio.gather() de tiet kiem thoi gian. Ket qua hoi tu tai Buoc 4.

BUOC 2: Slither — Phan Tich Tinh

slither_smart_wrapper.py 7 detectors
  • Phat hien phien ban: Doc pragma statement, xac dinh phien ban Solidity
  • Auto solc-select: Tu dong cai dat va chuyen doi trinh bien dich solc
  • Cross-process lock: File-based lock dam bao an toan khi multi-worker
  • Strip imports: Ghi nhan protection (ReentrancyGuard, onlyOwner) TRUOC khi xoa import de bien dich
  • 7 Detectors su dung: reentrancy-eth, reentrancy-no-eth, reentrancy-benign, reentrancy-events, unchecked-send, unchecked-lowlevel, unchecked-transfer
  • Graceful fallback: Neu Slither loi → tra ve WARNING ro rang (khong phai false "safe")
Xu ly protection bi strip:
Khi code goc co import @openzeppelin → modifier ReentrancyGuard bi xoa de Slither bien dich → Thong tin nay duoc ghi vao warnings[] de LLM biet code goc co bao ve.

BUOC 3: RAG — Tim Kiem Tri Thuc

smart_rag_system.py Qdrant DB
  • Per-function search: Moi ham rui ro duoc tim kiem rieng trong knowledge base
  • CodeRankEmbed: Nhung vector 768 chieu, su dung tien to "search_query:" cho truy van
  • Qdrant search: Cosine similarity, limit = top_k × 3 (du 3 lan cho reranker)
  • Score threshold: 0.3 (loai bo ket qua cosine similarity thap)
  • Dedup: Nhom theo (vuln_type, swc_id, source_function, audit_company), giu toi da 2-3 moi nhom
  • Asymmetric embedding: Document prefix "search_document:", Query prefix "search_query:" — thiet ke co chu dich cua CodeRankEmbed
Data flow:
Qdrant tra ve toi da 30 ket qua/ham → Cat giu top 10/ham → Gop tat ca N ham → Dedup → Con 10-25 ket qua → Chuyen sang Buoc 4

BUOC 4 — Cross-Encoder Reranking + CRAG Gate

Xep hang lai ket qua RAG bang cross-encoder, sau do ap dung cong CRAG de loc chat luong bang chung truoc khi gui cho LLM.

4a. Cross-Encoder Reranking

ms-marco-MiniLM-L-12-v2 33M params
  • Input: (rerank_query, document_text) pairs
  • rerank_query: Xay dung tu ham rui ro top-1 (ten ham, risk indicators, 300 ky tu code)
  • Document text: "Vulnerability: X | Root cause: Y | Code: Z"
  • Scoring: raw logits → sigmoid → [0, 1]
  • Combined score: 0.4 × bi_encoder + 0.6 × cross_encoder
  • Output: Top 5 ket qua, sap xep theo combined score
Tai sao ms-marco cho Solidity? Vi input cua reranker la van ban mo ta (NL + code), khong phai code thuan tuy. CodeRankEmbed (code-specialized) da loc o buoc 1 (bi-encoder). ms-marco xu ly tot van ban hon hop.

4b. CRAG Gate (Corrective RAG)

Rule-based KHONG phai ML
  • Nguon cam hung: Yan et al., arXiv:2401.15884
  • 3 hanh dong dua tren combined_score:
CORRECTscore ≥ 0.7Gui tat ca 5 ket qua cho LLM
AMBIGUOUS0.3 ≤ score < 0.7Chi gui ket qua co score ≥ 0.3
INCORRECTscore < 0.3KHONG gui bang chung — LLM tu phan doan
Bi-encoder floor: Neu CodeRankEmbed score ≥ 0.75 nhung combined < 0.3 → day len AMBIGUOUS (vi CodeRankEmbed hieu code tot hon).

BUOC 5 — LLM Chain-of-Thought Reasoning

Gui tat ca thong tin (code, Slither warnings, RAG evidence) den Gemini 2.5 Pro. Su dung Prompt Tiering va 14 quy tac chong ao giac.

llm_analyzer.py Gemini 2.5 Pro Chain-of-Thought

Prompt Tiering — Kien Truc Prompt 2 Tang:

TangNoi dungKhi nao co
Tier 1 Checklist kiem tra 3 loai lo hong co ban (SWC-107, SWC-101, SWC-104). Yeu cau tra loi YES/NO cho tung loai. Luon co — moi lan phan tich
Tier 2 Expert rules tu 458 audit cases: nhan dien safe patterns, calibration muc do nghiem trong, quy tac xac minh exploit. Chi khi RAG co bang chung — do luong dong gop cua RAG

Cau Truc Prompt Gui Den LLM:

  • Phan Slither: Danh sach canh bao tu Slither (hoac "No vulnerabilities detected" / "SLITHER UNAVAILABLE")
  • Phan RAG: Top 3 cases tuong tu voi root_cause, trigger_condition, fix_solution
  • Phan Expert Rules (Tier 2): Safe patterns, severity calibration, exploit verification — CHI KHI co RAG evidence
  • Phan Code: Toan bo ma nguon can phan tich
  • Phan Checklist (Tier 1): 3 loai lo hong kiem tra
  • 14 Quy tac chong ao giac: Dam bao LLM khong bao cao sai

14 Quy Tac Chong Ao Giac (Anti-Hallucination):

  • 1. Chi bao cao SWC-107, SWC-101, SWC-104
  • 2. .send()/.transfer() ≠ reentrancy (2300 gas)
  • 3. Solidity ≥ 0.8.0 → SWC-101 KHONG THE
  • 4. ERC20 .transfer() ≠ raw .call()
  • 5. Phai cung cap kich ban khai thac cu the
  • 6. SafeMath → SWC-101 an toan
  • 7. Bounded arithmetic → SWC-101 an toan
  • 8. ReentrancyGuard → SWC-107 an toan
  • 9. CEI pattern → SWC-107 an toan
  • 10. Return value checked → SWC-104 an toan
  • 11. Chi bao cao High/Critical severity
  • 12. Primary = nguy hiem nhat; secondary = phu
  • 13. Khong bao cao de an toan (precision > recall)
  • 14. Slither co false negative cao → verify bang code review

Hau Xu Ly (Post-Processing):

  • _filter_out_of_scope(): Loai bo moi SWC khong nam trong {SWC-107, SWC-101, SWC-104}
  • _filter_pragma_080(): Neu Solidity ≥ 0.8.0 → xoa tat ca SWC-101 (vi built-in overflow protection)
  • JSON parsing: Thu phan tich truc tiep → trich tu markdown block → tim { ... } ngoai cung
  • Retry: 3-5 lan voi exponential backoff (10s, 20s, 40s) khi gap rate limit

07 3 Loai Lo Hong Muc Tieu (SWC)

He thong tap trung phat hien 3 loai lo hong nghiem trong nhat trong smart contract Solidity.

SWC-107 — Reentrancy

Critical / High 138 cases in KB

Nguyen nhan goc: Goi ham ben ngoai (external call) truoc khi cap nhat trang thai (state update). Vi pham mau Checks-Effects-Interactions (CEI).

Kich ban khai thac: Ke tan cong trien khai hop dong doc hai goi lai ham withdraw() truoc khi so du duoc cap nhat.

Phong chong:

  • Su dung ReentrancyGuard / nonReentrant modifier
  • Ap dung mau CEI (Checks-Effects-Interactions)
  • Su dung mutex lock
  • .send()/.transfer() gioi han 2300 gas (an toan)

SWC-101 — Integer Overflow/Underflow

High / Medium 204 cases in KB

Nguyen nhan goc: Phep tinh so hoc khong duoc bao ve tren Solidity < 0.8.0. Bien uint256 tran khi vuot qua gia tri toi da.

Kich ban khai thac: Ke tan cong gui gia tri lon khien balance + amount tran ve 0.

Phong chong:

  • Su dung Solidity ≥ 0.8.0 (built-in overflow protection)
  • Su dung thu vien SafeMath (cho Solidity < 0.8.0)
  • Kiem tra bien truoc phep tinh (bounded arithmetic)
Luu y: Solidity ≥ 0.8.0 co bao ve tran so tu dong. Pipeline se TU DONG loai bo SWC-101 neu phat hien pragma ≥ 0.8.0.

SWC-104 — Unchecked Call Return Value

High / Medium 116 cases in KB

Nguyen nhan goc: Goi ham cap thap (.call(), .send()) ma khong kiem tra gia tri tra ve. Neu goi that bai, chuong trinh van tiep tuc.

Kich ban khai thac: Giao dich chuyen ETH that bai nhung contract van danh dau la da chuyen thanh cong.

Phong chong:

  • Dung require() kiem tra gia tri tra ve
  • Dung if() guard de xu ly loi
  • Su dung .transfer() (tu dong revert khi that bai)
